About Me

Yo! I'm Iyad, currently stuck in Yanbu (seriously, it's so boring here, I miss Medina so much 😭). But hey, at least I have Alien Stage to keep me sane! I'm absolutely OBSESSED with this masterpiece by VIVINOS × QMENG - like, unhealthily obsessed. When I'm not crying over the latest episode, you'll find me doodling random stuff, vibing to music that probably makes no sense to anyone else, or annoying my friends Noah, Milo, and Riyadh with my latest conspiracy theories about the series.

I'm basically just a chaotic art kid who discovered the perfect blend of music, animation, and emotional trauma packaged into beautiful episodes. My life motto? If it doesn't make me question my existence while looking aesthetically pleasing, I don't want it! 🌌✨
